About

I am a data science and quantitative finance professional based in Hong Kong, currently working as a Quantitative Risk Analyst at LIM Advisors. My background spans economics, financial risk management and machine learning. I hold the CFA charter, the Certificate in Quantitative Finance (CQF), and an MPhil in Economics from the University of Cambridge.

My work focuses on the practical application of quantitative methods — from measuring portfolio risk with Value at Risk and Expected Shortfall, to portfolio optimisation, Monte Carlo simulation and derivatives pricing. More recently I have been building LLM-powered agent systems, including multi-agent workflows, retrieval-augmented generation and natural-language interfaces to databases.

I enjoy sharing what I learn: I write about quantitative finance and data science on Medium, and publish the accompanying code on GitHub so others can reproduce and build on it.
